F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kind of surfaces can you print on?

    We can print on virtually any flat surface, like glass and more. If you're unsure if we can print on a particular surface, just contact us and we'll let you know.

  • What kind of ink do you use for printing?

    We use high-quality, eco-friendly and UV treated ink for all of our printing projects. Our ink is designed to produce vibrant, long-lasting colors.

  • How long does it take to complete a custom printing project?

    The timeline for a custom printing project can vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. However, we typically provide an estimated timeline for each project before starting work, so you will have a clear idea of when your project will be completed.

  • Can you help me with the design of my project?

    Yes! Our team of experienced designers can work with you to create a custom design that perfectly captures your vision. We will work closely with you to understand your needs and preferences, and will provide multiple design options for you to choose from.

  • What file formats do you accept for printing?

    We accept a wide range of file formats for printing, including PDF, EPS, AI, PSD, and JPEG. If you have a file format that is not listed here, please contact us to see if we are able to accept it.

  • What is your pricing structure for custom printing services?

    Our pricing structure for custom printing services varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. We provide detailed quotes for each project before starting work, so you will have a clear idea of the cost before committing to the project.
